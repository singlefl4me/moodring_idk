
Idea Adoption Jam 2025 entry

Coming home from a very stressful and tiring day, you stumble into your room to suddenly find a small box on your desk. Inside are a couple of rings that bore beautiful blue stones. You don't know how this box got here, maybe someone might've misplaced these. You should probably give them back- wait, did one of them just move?
